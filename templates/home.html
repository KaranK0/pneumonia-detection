Symptoms of Pneumonia

Common symptoms of pneumonia include coughing, chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, fever, and chills. In some cases, people with pneumonia may also experience headaches, muscle aches, and sweating.

Diagnosis and Treatment of Pneumonia

To detect pneumonia, a doctor may perform a physical exam, order chest X-rays or CT scans, and conduct blood tests or sputum cultures to identify the specific microorganism causing the infection. In some cases, a bronchoscopy may be necessary to examine the airways
